What is Magic: The Gathering

Created in 1993, MAGIC: THE GATHERING is the world's first trading card game. Players take turns battling one another by casting spells, summoning creatures and using artifacts depicted on individual cards drawn from their personalized decks. Highly strategic gameplay that is customizable to any individual's play style.

What is Commander in Magic: The Gathering?

Also known as Elder Dragon Highlander (EDH), is a unique and social multiplayer variant of the game of Magic The Gathering. In Commander, each player constructs a deck consisting of 100 cards, led by a legendary creature designated as their "commander." Only one copy of each card (excluding basic lands) is allowed in the deck. The commander starts the game in the command zone and can be cast from there, but if it's sent to the graveyard or exiled, it can be recast for an additional cost. Players begin with 40 life, and games are typically played with four players. The format encourages creativity and personal expression in deck building, fostering a diverse and often unpredictable gaming experience. Additionally, there is a restricted list of cards that are banned or limited in Commander to maintain balance and enhance the overall enjoyment of the format. Commander emphasizes social interaction, multiplayer dynamics, and the joy of playing with iconic legendary creatures from Magic's vast history.
